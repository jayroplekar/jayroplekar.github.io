Based on my research, the job roles most heavily impacted by AI can be categorized as follows:

**High Potential for Automation:**
*   **Administrative and Clerical:** Data entry, secretarial work, and postal services.
*   **Customer Service:** Routine inquiries and support.
*   **Manufacturing and Warehouse:** Assembly, packaging, and inventory management.
*   **Finance and Insurance:** Tellers and clerks.
*   **Procurement:** Clerical and transactional roles.

**Augmented by AI:**
*   **Creative Professions:** Artists, designers, and writers using AI as a tool.
*   **Software Development:** Code generation, testing, and debugging.
*   **Legal Professionals:** Document review, contract analysis, and legal research.
*   **Marketing:** Content creation, customer segmentation, and data analysis.
*   **Healthcare:** Medical image analysis, diagnosis, and treatment planning.

**New Roles Created by AI:**
*   **AI and Machine Learning Specialists:** Developers and implementers of AI systems.
*   **Data Analysts and Scientists:** Interpreting data generated by AI.
*   **AI-Generated Content Specialists:** Prompting and refining AI-generated content.
*   **Human-Machine Teaming Managers:** Overseeing human-AI collaboration.
*   **AI Quality Controllers and Editors:** Ensuring responsible and ethical AI use.

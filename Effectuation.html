Effectuation

Prof. Sarasvathy, is a high energy and engaging presenter. Based on her in-depth research of successful entrepreneurs, she persuasively describes a framework for entrepreunership behaviors called Effectuation. The Key idea is the successful Entrepreneurs shape the world around for better, with a scale of their choosing rather than predicting/planning the future to precision.

Key Ideas & My Take

They consistently deploy the following five behaviors:
  1. Means
    2. (Bird in Hand: start small with what you have i.e., abilities, expeience and network instead of a super detailed plan.)
  2. Affordable Loss
    3. ( Instead of maximizing or expecting a return, use "is the loss affordable" to get off center and generate options for the future)
  3. Co-Creation Partnership
    4. (Focus on creating parterships with nearest customer, supplier, stakeholder and get their "pre-commitment" instead of obsessing over beating the competition. My Take: Story of Tesla's founding in Walter Issacson's Elon Musk bio on how Elon Musk, JB Straubel, AC Propulsion, Martin Eberhardt got introduced is a very interesting example relevant to this. May be another thought on this later ..)
  4. Leverage Contingencies
    5. (Make lemonade if come across lemons, reminds me of Rahm Emannuel's "Don't let A Crisis go to waste")
  5. Worldview
    6. (Control/shape the future instead of focusing on prediction)

My Take

while most of the discusssion is centered on Enterpreunership, it applies to intrapreuners as well with right cautions/qualifiers, YMMV
